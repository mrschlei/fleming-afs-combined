Regents Room Use Policy

  • The group using the room is responsible for ensuring the room is left exactly as it was found.
  • The table in the Regents room takes special care do not use any chemicals or wet rags on the table and wipe the table with a dry cloth. Wipe the place mats and coasters off with a damp cloth; do not remove the placemats or the coasters from the table. The microphones stay plugged in on the table.
  • Audio visual equipment (projector, screen, PowerPoint setup, on-line access) is available in the Regents’ Room. Please contact Neighborhood IT at 615-3336 or isssemail@umich.edu.

Since the Regents Room must remain locked at all times when not in use, please let us know if your meeting is cancelled.
