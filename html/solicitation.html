Solicitation

The Fleming building allows solicitation for donations (items) to a charity with the below procedures followed:

  1. Must be sponsored by a Fleming Department. FLEMDOC email cannot be used to solicit donations.
  2. Notify the Fleming Building Manager before setting up a drop box in the building.
  3. Collection location limited to kitchenette area. Box must be neat in appearance with function and sponsoring department clearly stated.
  4. Pickups should occur regular so as to minimize theft & prevent overflow.
  5. Drive lasts a maximum of 2 weeks.

The Fleming building does not allow money collection boxes/containers.

The Fleming Building staff puts on a Holiday potluck in December and chooses a charity to make donations. If you are interested in nominating a charity for this event please contact Anita Greca for current chair information.
