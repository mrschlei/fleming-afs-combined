2015 Fleming Wellness Room Guidelines

  1. The Fleming Wellness room is for Fleming Building Employees. Fleming employees may bring a guest under the following conditions:
    1. Only one guest at a time.
    2. Guest must be a U-M employee and accompanied by a Fleming employee that remains in the wellness room at all times.
    3. Fleming employee member is responsible for ensuring all rules are followed by their guest.
    4. Prior to first use, guest must meet the requirements set forth below in section B.
  2. Prior to obtaining access to or using the Wellness room, all users, including visitors, must complete the following:
    1. All users will visit the wellness room orientation website to familiarize themselves with the safe use of equipment:
      http://fleming.bf.umich.edu/video.html
    2. All users shall read, date and sign a "Request for Access & Release of Liability" form to be maintained by AVPFO office. Annually, all users are required to renew the liability form. You will be notified by the building manager when it is time to renew the form.
    3. Electronic access will be granted by Building Manager via your M-Card, only after the orientation program is complete. Guests will not be provided access through their M-Card.
  3. Fleming building student employees have restricted access to the Wellness room.
    1. Fleming student employees hours are Monday - Friday from 6am to 6pm.
    2. Fleming student employees may not bring a guest.
  4. Wellness room is open 24/7.
  5. Use of the Wellness room, at any time, is on personal time.
  6. Use of the Fleming Wellness room is at your own risk.
  7. Consult your physician before beginning any exercise program.
  8. Exercise with a buddy.
  9. When using the weight machine a spotter is highly recommended.
  10. Each time before you use a piece of equipment, inspect it as you have been trained. Should you notice damaged or defective equipment, DO NOT USE IT. Immediately tag the equipment with an "Out of Order" sign (signs by phone) and notify the Building Manager as soon as possible.
  11. The Fleming Building Manager must be notified of all injuries and/or medical emergencies as soon as possible:
    1. Medical emergencies: Dial 911 from phone in the room.
    2. For non-emergencies and minor injuries: A first aid kit is available in the Wellness room; follow-up with your own doctor.
  12. Wellness room rules:
    1. Please keep the room clean and orderly.
    2. Food is prohibited. Beverages must be in a non-spillable drinking cup.
    3. Wipe down equipment after each use.
    4. Please be considerate of others waiting to use the machines.
    5. All equipment must stay in the room and returned to proper location.
    6. Wellness room door shall remain locked at all times. Do not prop the door open.
    7. Do not leave valuables unprotected. The U-M accepts no responsibility for lost or stolen articles.
      • Found keys go to the Fleming Building Manager.
      • Lost and found items go to Mailroom/Information Desk.

Lockers

The following addendum is effective 9/9/2013 and is in addition to the Fleming Wellness Room Guidelines dated 2013.

There are a total of 14 lockers. 12 lockers with combinations will be available for assignment. 2 lockers with removable keys will be available for temporary use during workouts; items must be removed at end of workout. The U-M accepts no responsibility for securing items and does not provide reimbursement for lost or stolen property. The Building Manager will retain master keys and combinations.

  • To be eligible for a locker you must be a Fleming building employee.
  • Fleming student employees are ineligible for the locker lottery.
  • Locker assignments will be through an annual lottery with locker assignment from January through December.
  • The Fleming Wellness Committee will manage the locker lottery. All eligible building employees requesting a locker will be assigned a number using the random number generator, with the first 12 (1-12) receiving a locker. If a locker becomes available during the year, the locker will be assigned to the next person on the wait list (number 13, then 14, etc.). After the lottery, eligible staff may be added to the bottom of the wait list. To enter the lottery or to be added to the bottom of the waitlist contact flemingwellnesschampions@umich.edu.
  • Lockers are to be emptied by the last working day of the year; new locker assignments will be available for on the 2nd work day after the holiday in January.
  • Locker combination is NON-TRANSFERABLE.
  • Due to the potential for bugs and other pests, food may not be stored in the locker.

Showers

  • Door must be locked when in use.
  • Clothing and other personal items (shampoo, ect.) may not be stored in the shower room at any time.
  • Please leave shower room in clean condition.
