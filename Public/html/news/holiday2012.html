2012 Holiday Break Building Information

PLEASE SHARE THIS WITH ALL YOUR STAFF:

The Fleming Building exterior doors will be locked at the end of the day on Friday, December 21st and will unlock at 7:30 am on Wednesday, January 2nd. *Please note the Fleming Building doors will be locked on Monday, Dec. 24th.

If you come in during the break please make sure to have your MCard and tightly close the doors behind you on the way in and out. The building ventilation system will be lowered over the break. If you plan to come in during the break please dress appropriately.

The power will be shut off to the building on Thursday, Dec. 27th from 7 am to about 9 am. This is due to the Institute for Social Research (ISR) renovation project and another project to remove ISR and Fleming from the same power feed.

Regular custodial services will conclude the evening of Thursday, Dec. 20 and will resume on Wednesday, January 2. For trash generated on Friday, Dec. 21 please place in the kitchenettes for a trash pickup during the break.

In order to help maintain a safe and secure environment, please remember the following tips

  • Confirm thermostats are set as they are labeled;
    • Interior space (single) thermostats: 74
    • Exterior office (dual) thermostats: Red - 68 & Blue - 74
  • Turn off all unused appliances, including coffee pots, heaters, computers, printers, copiers and lights to avoid over-heating and reduce electrical usage;
  • Secure moveable equipment such as LCDs and laptops;
  • Lock any personal or confidential items in secure locations;
  • Lock your desk;
  • Close and lock all outside windows, offices and closets to prevent broken pipes and thefts.

During the holidays, please call:

  • 911 for emergencies;
  • 763-1131 to reach the University Police for non-emergencies;
  • 647-2059 to reach Plant Operations Call Center for physical plant maintenance requests.

Have a safe and happy holiday season!
