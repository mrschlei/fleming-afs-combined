2018 Fleming Wellness Room Guidelines

Wellness Room Rules

  1. Always keep the Wellness Room clean and orderly.
  2. Food is prohibited.
  3. Beverages must be in a spill-proof container.
  4. Wipe down equipment after each use.
  5. Always be considerate of others waiting to use machines.
  6. All equipment must stay in the Wellness Room and be returned to the proper location after use.
  7. The Wellness Room door is to remain locked at all times. Do not prop the door open.
  8. Do not leave valuables unprotected. U-M accepts no liability or responsibility for lost or stolen articles.
    • Found keys go to the Fleming Building Manager.
    • Lost and found items go to Mailroom/Information Desk.

Guidelines

Wellness Room:

  1. The Fleming Wellness room is for Fleming Building Employees and Fleming Building Student-Employees only and allowed guests.
  2. Prior to obtaining access to or using the Wellness room, all Fleming Building Employees and Fleming Building Student-Employees that wish to use the Wellness Room, including allowed guests, must complete the following:
    1. All users will visit the Wellness Room orientation website to familiarize themselves with the safe use of the equipment. Website: http://fleming.bf.umich.edu/
    2. All users shall read, date and sign a Request for Access & Release of Liability form to be maintained by the AVPFO office. Annually, all users are required to renew the liability form. You will be notified by the Building Manager when it is time to renew the form.
    3. Electronic access will be granted by the Building Manager via your M-Card only after the orientation program is complete. Guests will not be provided access through their M-Card.
  3. Fleming Building Employees may bring a guest to the Wellness Room under the following conditions only:
    1. Only one guest for each Fleming Building Employee is allowed at a time.
    2. The guest must also be a U-M employee and be accompanied by the Fleming Building Employee at all times while in the Wellness Room.
    3. The Fleming Building Employee is responsible for ensuring all rules are followed by the guest.
    4. Prior to the first use of the Wellness Room by a guest, that guest will have gone through and completed the requirements of Section B above.
  4. Fleming Building Student-Employees have restricted access to the Wellness Room.
    1. Fleming Building Student-Employees may use the Wellness Room Monday – Friday from 6am to 6pm.
    2. Fleming Building Student-Employees may not bring a guest into the Wellness Room.
  5. The Wellness Room is open 24/7.
  6. Use of the Wellness Room, at any time, is on personal time.
  7. Use of the Wellness Room is always at your own risk.
  8. Consult your physician before beginning any exercise program.
  9. Always exercise with a buddy.
  10. When using the weight machine, a spotter is highly recommended.
  11. Each time before you use a piece of equipment, inspect it as you have been trained. Should you notice damaged or defective equipment, DO NOT USE IT. Immediately tag the equipment with an "Out of Order" sign (located by the telephone) and immediately notify the Building Manager.
  12. The Building Manager must be notified of all injuries and/or medical emergencies as soon as possible:
    1. Medical emergencies: Dial 911 from the telephone in the room.
    2. For non-emergencies and minor injuries: A first aid kit is available in the Wellness Room. Always follow-up with your own doctor as needed.

Lockers:

There are 14 lockers available to Wellness Room users. Two of the lockers have removable keys and are available for temporary use during workouts; however, items must be removed at end of a workout. The remaining twelve lockers have combination locks and are assigned by a random lottery to those users wishing to utilize these lockers. U-M accepts no responsibility for securing items and does not provide reimbursement for lost or stolen property. The Building Manager will retain master keys and combinations to all fourteen lockers.

  • To be eligible for one of the twelve randomly-assigned lockers, you must be a Fleming Building Employee. Fleming Building Student-Employees are not eligible for the locker lottery.
  • Locker assignments are made through an annual lottery with locker assignments running from January through December of each year.
  • The Fleming Wellness Committee will manage the locker lottery. All eligible Fleming Building Employees requesting a locker will be assigned a number using a random number generator. The first twelve (that is, those users randomly assigned numbers 1-12) will receive a locker for the year. If a locker becomes available during the year, the locker will be assigned to the next person on the waiting list (that is, the user that is assigned number 13, then 14, etc.). After the lottery, eligible Fleming Building Employees may be added to the bottom of the waiting list. To enter the lottery, or to be added to the bottom of the waiting list, contact flemingwellnesschampions@umich.edu.
  • Lockers are to be emptied by the last working day of the year.
  • New locker assignments will be available for use on the 2nd working day in January.
  • Lockers are to be utilized by the assigned user only and are NON-TRANSFERABLE. If you wish to discontinue use of a locker that has been assigned to you, it will be given to the next user on the waiting list.
  • Due to the potential for bugs and other pests, food may not be stored in any locker.

Showers:

  • Door must be locked when in use.
  • Clothing and other personal items (shampoo, etc.) may not be stored in the shower room at any time.
  • Always leave the shower room in a clean condition.
