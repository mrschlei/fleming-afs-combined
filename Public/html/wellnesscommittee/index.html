Fleming Wellness Committee

Mission

The University of Michigan Fleming Wellness Committee (FWC) is comprised of staff members that represent various executive offices within the Fleming Administration building. The FWC enthusiastically supports and strives to carry out the mission of the University MHealthy Program; and serves as a conduit to inform, provide and promote health and well being of the Fleming building occupants.

Goal

The goal of the FWC is to promote greater health awareness and provide information, activities, and services designed to support healthy lifestyle choices. The FWC is dedicated to encouraging general health and well being of the Fleming Building occupants as well as creating opportunities for staff to engage in planned programs/activities on campus and with others in the building.

The FWC also encourages feedback from the building occupants regarding previous events and solicits ideas for future programs.
